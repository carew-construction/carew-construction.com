Who I Am

Rooted in Sacramento.
Built on Honest Work.

I'm Ben Carew — a one-man operation built on one simple idea: do the job right, treat people with respect, and stand behind the work. At Carew Construction, I treat every customer like family.

Whether it's a repair that's been on your list for months, a project you've been putting off, or something that just broke today — I handle it. No job is too small. No runaround. Just solid work from someone who takes pride in it.

I serve homeowners and property managers throughout the Greater Sacramento area, including Citrus Heights, Elk Grove, Roseville, Rancho Cordova, Folsom, and surrounding communities.
