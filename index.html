Who We Are

Rooted in Sacramento.
Built on Honest Work.

Carew Construction is a Sacramento-area handyman and construction service built on one simple idea: do the job right, treat people with respect, and stand behind the work.

Whether it's a repair that's been on your list for months, a project you've been putting off, or something that just broke and needs fixing today — we handle it. No job is too small. No runaround. Just solid work from someone who takes pride in it.

We serve homeowners and property managers throughout the Greater Sacramento area, including Citrus Heights, Elk Grove, Roseville, Rancho Cordova, Folsom, and the surrounding communities.
